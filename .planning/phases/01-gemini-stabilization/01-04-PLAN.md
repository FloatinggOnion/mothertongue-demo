<objective>
Replace all message ID generation from Date.now() to crypto.randomUUID() to eliminate collision risk and ensure message IDs are cryptographically unique across concurrent requests.

Purpose: INFRA-03 requires that message IDs cannot collide. Date.now() has only millisecond resolution and can collide under parallel requests. crypto.randomUUID() generates RFC 4122 v4 UUIDs with 122 bits of entropy.

Output: All message IDs in drill/[id]/page.tsx use crypto.randomUUID(); timestamps remain Date.now().
</objective>
